About Us

DGDs SENSES Your Salon and Spa is a

DGDs SENSES Your Salon and Spa is a locally-established salon mainly located in Rd1 Minuyan Proper San Jose Del Monte, Bulacan. The salon is owned and operated by Dennis David a Marketing and Business Administration graduate with 10 years experience in Corporate Marketing and Sales in Metro Manila. DGDs SENSES Your Salon and Spa humbly began January 8, 2012. Dennis opens the pioneering outlet with a goal of serving the customer with the best of quality and customer service. The salon aims to deliver service that will pamper the customer in achieving a more beautiful and relaxing feeling. DGDs SENSES Your Salon and Spa offers hair treatments, massage, body scrub and many more. The company wants the customers to feel good and look good as well. The salon is not limited to women clientele; in fact most men prefer also the touch of DGDs SENSES Your Salon and Spa to achieve maximum beauty with affordable price. The salon is staffed with well-trained and friendly service provider that will serve the customers with maximum satisfaction. The salon implemented a specialized training for employees that will prepare them in meeting the standards of customers.
